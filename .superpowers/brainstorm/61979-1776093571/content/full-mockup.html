How it works
Recycler helps you clean up your Solana wallet and earn SMELT rewards
1
Scan
Connect your wallet. Recycler checks all your token accounts and flags any under $0.10 as recyclable.
2
Select & Recycle
Review the list, approve one transaction, and reclaim ~0.002 SOL per closed account (minus 5% fee).
3
Earn SMELT
250 SMELT minted to your wallet for every account closed. Hold SMELT to earn weekly SOL distributions.
